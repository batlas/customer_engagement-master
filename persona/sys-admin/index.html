Lehani Tuamotu
IBM Support Engineer Level 2

“I spend a lot of my time just documenting what I did. I work with multiple clients on multiple tickets at the same time. I am constantly switching back and forth between different problems and different screens. It can be challenging to keep track of my time. I prefer to communicate with clients via email because then I have a record of what I told them. Even though talking to them on the phone is often more effective.”

Goals

  • Help as many clients as I can as fast as I can.
  • Find the solution to the ticket quickly.
  • Remain calm and professional when customer is frustrated.
  • Develop expertise with the technology.

Tasks

  • Take ownership of ticket.
  • Reviews customer logs and files.
  • Respond to client.
    • Email
    • Phone
    • Chat
  • Search for solution.
  • Document time spent on ticket.
  • Document activities done to resolve ticket.
  • Follow up with clients.
  • Test possible solutions.
  • Ask for help from other support engineers.
  • Escalate and transfer tickets.

Tools

  • Retain
  • Scratch Pads
  • Email
  • Phone
  • Sametime
  • IBM Knowledge Base

Team

  • Other L2's on her team
  • L3 engineer
  • Client

Pain

  • It is hard to keep track of how much time I spent on each ticket.
  • I spend so much time following the processes to track my efficiency that it significantly impacts my efficiency.
  • Calling clients creates double the amount of work because I have to remember to write down everything I said and did on the phone.
  • Lack of common knowledge base.
  • Tools differ across teams -- making it difficult to access information.
  • There is difficulty finding solutions from past tickets.
  • Queue descriptions/logs are long & confusing.
  • Time consuming to update customers.
  • Frustrating for Mike as all of his work is quantified by how quickly he can perform a task.

Needs

  • Streamlined processes.
  • Reminders to know when I need to follow-up with a client.
  • Easier ways to track my time and document my work.
  • Needs to collaborate with the best of the right people.
  • Unified tools within/across teams.
  • Transparent communication within teams/team members.
